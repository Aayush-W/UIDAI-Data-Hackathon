## üéØ Purpose of Geographic Heatmaps

These heatmaps allow UIDAI to:

- Instantly identify **high-risk geographic clusters**
- Distinguish **persistent vs emerging risk zones**
- Understand **why** a district is at risk, not just **where**
- Align interventions geographically rather than uniformly
